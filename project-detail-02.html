Project Overview

AVANTI Research is a corporate and e-commerce platform providing market research products and services. The project focused on rebuilding and optimizing the frontend architecture to improve usability, performance, and scalability across product listing and checkout flows.

My Role & Contributions

  • Developed Product Listing Pages (PLP) and Product Detail Pages (PDP)
  • Implemented end-to-end order workflow including checkout and order management

  • Optimized performance and improved accessibility compliance

Technologies
